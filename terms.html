4. Nature of the Service

CodeBluePediatrics is a clinical decision support tool that provides evidence-based clinical information, specialist-routed guidance, drug dosing calculations, clinical calculator results, equipment sizing references, and literature references in response to clinical questions submitted via text message.

The Service uses artificial intelligence models developed by third parties (currently Anthropic, PBC) to generate responses. These AI-generated responses are processed through multiple automated safety validation layers, including:

Despite these safety measures, the Service relies on AI-generated content and is inherently subject to the limitations of such technology, including the possibility of errors, omissions, inaccuracies, outdated information, and hallucinated content. No automated safety system can eliminate all risk of incorrect clinical information.
