Privacy Policy

Service: CodeBluePediatrics  ·  Operator: Celeritas Health LLC  ·  Last Updated: June 6, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Celeritas Health LLC ("Celeritas Health," "we," "us") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with CodeBluePediatrics (the "Service"), a clinical decision support platform that provides pediatric clinical guidance to licensed healthcare providers via SMS and WhatsApp messaging.

The Service is intended only for licensed healthcare providers. It is not directed to, and is not intended for use by, the general public, patients, or children.

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to deliver and operate the Service:

CategoryWhat it is and how we handle it
Message contentThe text of clinical questions a provider voluntarily sends. Inbound messages are automatically de-identified before any AI processing or storage using the HIPAA Safe Harbor method (see below).
Mobile numberThe sending phone number is immediately converted to a one-way cryptographic hash (HMAC-SHA256) used only for rate limiting and abuse prevention. The raw number is not stored and the hash is automatically purged within approximately two hours.
Service metadataDe-identified operational data such as timestamps, which protocol matched, response latency, and delivery status — used to operate, secure, and improve the Service.

We do not collect or retain Protected Health Information (PHI), and we do not build marketing or advertising profiles of any user.

De-Identification of Messages

Before any inbound message is processed by AI or written to storage, the Service applies the HIPAA Safe Harbor de-identification method (45 CFR § 164.514(b)(2)), programmatically removing the 18 categories of identifiers (names, geographic detail, dates, contact information, account and device identifiers, and similar). De-identification is best-effort and automated; the Service is designed so that no PHI is retained, and providers are asked not to include directly identifying patient information in messages.

How We Use Information

  • Service delivery — to match a clinical question to the appropriate protocol and return a response.
  • Safety monitoring — to detect and prevent unsafe outputs, abuse, and system failures.
  • Quality improvement and analytics — using de-identified, aggregate data to improve accuracy, performance, and reliability.
  • Legal and security — to comply with applicable law and protect the integrity and security of the Service.

How We Share Information

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes — ever. Mobile information is never shared with third parties or affiliates for their marketing or promotional purposes.

We share information only with the service providers (subprocessors) strictly necessary to operate the Service, and only to the extent required to provide it:

  • Messaging delivery — to transmit SMS/WhatsApp messages to and from providers.
  • AI processing — to generate clinical decision support responses from de-identified message content.
  • Cloud hosting — to host and run the Service.

These providers are bound by contractual confidentiality and data-protection obligations and may use the information only to provide services to Celeritas Health. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and the security of the Service.

Data Retention

Hashed mobile numbers are automatically purged within approximately two hours. De-identified clinical content used for an active session is retained only transiently and subject to short, automated time-to-live limits. De-identified, non-identifying operational and audit records may be retained longer to support safety, reliability, and legal compliance. We retain information no longer than necessary for the purposes described in this Policy.

Your Choices and Opt-Out

Messaging is always provider-initiated and entirely voluntary. You can stop receiving messages at any time:

  • Reply STOP to any message to immediately unsubscribe.
  • Reply HELP for support information.
  • Email support@celeritashealth.com to request removal.

Message and data rates may apply, and message frequency varies based on your use. Consent to messaging is never a condition of any purchase or service. For complete opt-in and opt-out details, see the SMS & WhatsApp Messaging Consent page.

Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information, including transport encryption, one-way hashing of mobile numbers, automated de-identification, and least-privilege access controls. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but the Service is architected to minimize the data it holds.

Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for licensed healthcare providers and is not directed to children or to the general public. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the Service.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date above and, where appropriate, communicated through the Service.
