About the Service

CodeBluePediatrics is a clinical decision support platform operated by Celeritas Health LLC, a South Dakota limited liability company. The Service provides specialist-level pediatric clinical guidance to licensed healthcare providers via SMS and WhatsApp messaging.

The Service is designed for physicians, nurse practitioners, physician assistants, and other credentialed clinicians practicing in settings where pediatric subspecialty consultation is not readily available.

Voluntary Messaging Consent

Messaging consent is entirely voluntary. Opting in to receive SMS or WhatsApp messages from CodeBluePediatrics is not a condition of purchasing any goods or services from Celeritas Health LLC. You are never required to provide your phone number or consent to messaging.

Healthcare providers who wish to receive clinical decision support via text messaging may voluntarily opt in by following the process described below. Providers who prefer not to receive text messages are under no obligation to do so.

How Providers Voluntarily Opt In

Messaging is always initiated by the healthcare provider. The Service does not send unsolicited outbound messages. The voluntary opt-in process works as follows:

1
Provider voluntarily texts the service number. A healthcare provider who wishes to use the Service sends an initial text message (SMS or WhatsApp) to the CodeBluePediatrics number. This is a voluntary action — no one is required to text this number.
2
Disclosure is presented. The system responds with information about the Service, including that it is a clinical decision support tool, that messages are de-identified before processing, and that no Protected Health Information is stored.
3
Provider chooses to opt in. The provider may reply AGREE to voluntarily consent to receiving clinical decision support messages. This is a separate, voluntary action — the provider may choose not to reply and will receive no further messages.
4
Messages begin only after voluntary consent. After the provider has opted in, they may send clinical questions and will receive evidence-based responses via the same messaging channel. The provider may opt out at any time.

Providers who do not reply AGREE will not receive any additional messages. Consent is never assumed, inherited, or obtained through any mechanism other than the provider's own voluntary action.

How to Opt Out

Providers may stop receiving messages at any time, for any reason, using any of the following methods:

  • Reply STOP to any message from CodeBluePediatrics. This immediately unsubscribes the phone number from all future messages.
  • Reply HELP to receive support information and contact details.
  • Email support@celeritashealth.com to request removal.
  • Simply stop texting the service number. Because messages are reply-based, no further messages will be sent if you stop initiating contact.

After opting out via STOP, no further messages will be sent to that phone number. Opting out is immediate and carries no penalty or consequence of any kind. A provider may re-enroll at any time by texting the service number again and completing the voluntary opt-in process.

Privacy & Data Handling

The Service implements the HIPAA Safe Harbor method of de-identification (45 CFR § 164.514(b)(2)). All 18 categories of identifiers are automatically removed from inbound messages before AI processing or data storage. Phone numbers are immediately converted to one-way cryptographic hashes (HMAC-SHA256) that are automatically purged within 2 hours. No Protected Health Information is retained.

De-identified usage data is used for service delivery, quality improvement, safety monitoring, and aggregate analytics. The Company does not sell, rent, or share any user data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

For complete privacy and data handling details, see the Terms of Service, Sections 10–13.
