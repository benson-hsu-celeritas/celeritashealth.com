The Founder

Built by the specialist on the other end of the phone

As a PICU attending in rural America, I've been the specialist that physicians call at 2 AM when a child is deteriorating and they need help now. I know what those calls sound like — the urgency, the limited resources, the clinician doing their best with what they have.

The bottleneck isn't knowledge. It's access. The clinical guidance that saves children's lives shouldn't require a transfer line, a callback, or luck. It should be as immediate as texting a colleague.

CodeBluePediatrics takes what I do on those calls and makes it available to every clinician who needs it, through the device already in their pocket. We're not replacing specialists. We're extending their reach.

Currently building an advisory board in pediatric critical care and health AI.
The Opportunity

Free for clinicians. Built to scale.

CodeBluePediatrics is free for every clinician who uses it — physicians, APPs, anyone making pediatric clinical decisions. Revenue comes from health system partnerships that layer institutional protocols on top of the platform's clinical knowledge.

Two markets, one platform: HIPAA-compliant clinical consultation for U.S. clinicians, and educational reference content internationally via WhatsApp across 195 countries.

Pediatrics is where we start. The architecture is built to extend to any specialty, any setting, anywhere a clinician needs expert guidance and doesn't have it.

Global from day one

Network effects at every layer
