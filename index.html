The Founder

Built by the specialist on the other end of the phone

As a PICU attending in rural America, I've been the specialist that physicians call at 2 AM when a child is deteriorating and they need help now. I know what those calls sound like — the urgency, the limited resources, the clinician doing their best with what they have.

The bottleneck isn't knowledge. It's access. The clinical guidance that saves children's lives shouldn't require a transfer line, a callback, or luck. It should be as immediate as texting a colleague — because that's already how clinicians seek help.

CodeBluePediatrics takes what I do on those calls and makes it available to every clinician who needs it, across 195 countries, through the device already in their pocket. We're not replacing specialists. We're extending their reach.
