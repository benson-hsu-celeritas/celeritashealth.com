Why we exist

You cannot credibly judge clinical AI unless you have built one and lived with its failure modes. So we do both — we build it to a standard, and we research how everyone else's should be tested.

01

What we believe

One engineering doctrine runs through everything Celeritas touches — the build and the research alike.

→ ASSUME FAILURE

Design for the failure mode first. The dangerous failure is a confident wrong answer.

→ MEASURE THE DANGER

Not "how often right" but "how often confidently wrong" — on tests a system can genuinely fail.

→ ENFORCE IN CODE

Safety-critical behavior is a deterministic code path, not a polite request to a model.

→ NO SELF-GRADING

Let no model grade its own work. Independent review, or it doesn't ship.

→ DOCUMENT LIMITS

Known blind spots are written down and tested as known failures — not discovered by the user.
